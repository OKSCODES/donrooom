# Property Owner Manual

Property owners use `/property` to manage a single ownership-scoped accommodation operation.

## Onboarding

Complete the property profile, provide accurate contact and location information, add amenities, create rooms, upload representative images and submit the property for approval. Only approved properties are publicly discoverable.

## Inventory and availability

Maintain room capacity, pricing and status. Use Calendar and Bookings to avoid conflicts. Booking availability is rechecked transactionally at confirmation; owners must not create parallel offline allocations without updating availability.

## Guest service

Respond to booking requests promptly, update stay status accurately and reply professionally to reviews. Do not place sensitive guest information in public fields or image metadata.

## Images

Use JPEG, PNG or WebP images below 5 MB. Upload only content the business is authorized to publish. Remove outdated or misleading photographs.
