# Future Roadmap

## Near term

- Firebase App Check enforcement.
- Rules-emulator integration tests in CI.
- Verified property onboarding and moderation service levels.
- Production email provider and consent-aware analytics dashboards.

## Growth

- Server-side payment and refund orchestration.
- Multi-property organizations and staff permissions.
- Search indexing service for larger catalogues.
- Localization completion for Khasi and Hindi content.

## Scale

- Event-driven Cloud Functions for notifications, denormalized analytics and scheduled maintenance.
- Data warehouse exports, business intelligence and fraud signals.
- Regional deployment strategy and documented service-level objectives.

Roadmap work must preserve backward compatibility, privacy and least-privilege access.
