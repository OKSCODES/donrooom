# User Manuals

## Guest
Register, verify email, search properties, choose a room and dates, confirm the summary, and track the booking under Guest Bookings. Receipts can be viewed, printed, or downloaded. Favorites, recently viewed stays, reviews, notifications, profile data, and preferences are available in the Guest Dashboard.

## Property Owner
An administrator must promote the account to Property Owner. Complete the property profile, upload optimized images, add at least two rooms, configure amenities and availability, then submit the property for approval. Manage inquiries, booking decisions, arrivals, departures, reviews, and operating settings from the Property Dashboard.

## Administrator
Administrators manage users, role promotion, property approvals, rooms, bookings, reviews, platform taxonomy, notifications, reports, analytics, site settings, security, and audit logs. Destructive and privilege-changing actions should always be independently reviewed.
