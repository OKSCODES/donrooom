# Code of Conduct

DONROOM contributors must maintain a respectful, inclusive and professional environment. Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, abusive language and deliberate disruption are not accepted in project spaces.

## Expected behaviour

- Discuss technical decisions respectfully and with evidence.
- Protect user, property and booking data.
- Report security issues privately rather than publishing exploits.
- Accept review feedback and disclose conflicts of interest.
- Follow applicable law and platform policies.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions, restrict participation, or permanently ban participants whose conduct threatens people, data or the project. Incidents should be reported to the security contact listed in `SECURITY.md`. Reports are handled confidentially and without retaliation.
